Accurate Breadboard Connection Map:
  • Power Rails: Left (+5V, GND) and Right (+5V, GND) rails are connected horizontally
  • MQ-137 Sensor: VCC → +5V Rail | GND → GND Rail | AO → Arduino A0
  • LCD Display: VCC → +5V | GND → GND | SDA (A4) → Arduino A4 | SCL (A5) → Arduino A5
  • DFPlayer Mini: VCC → +5V | GND → GND | TX → Arduino D10 | RX (1kΩ) → Arduino D11
  • Relay Module: VCC → +5V | GND → GND | IN → Arduino D7 | COM → Power Bank USB
  • LED Indicators: Green (D5) & Red (D6) with 220Ω resistors to GND
  • Mist Maker: (+) → Relay NO | (−) → GND
